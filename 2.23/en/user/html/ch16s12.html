16.12. Visualize a chart as a pivot table or a map

When you have made a chart you can switch between pivot table, chart and map visualization of your data. The function is inactive if the data the chart is based on cannot render as a pivot table or map.

16.12.1. Open a chart as a pivot table

  1. Click Chart > Open this chart as table.

    Your current chart opens as a pivot table.

16.12.2. Open a chart as a map

  1. Click Chart > Open this chart as map.

    Your current chart opens as a map.
