2.3. Installing Apps into DHIS2

Apps can be installed by uploading zip file into the App Manager. In, Services → Apps, click on the App Store menu item. The app can be uploaded by pressing the Browse button and after selecting the zip package, the file is uploaded automatically and installed in DHIS2. You can also browse through apps in the DHIS2 app store and download apps from there. The DHIS2 app store allows for app searching, reviewing, commenting, requesting features, rating on the apps by the community.
