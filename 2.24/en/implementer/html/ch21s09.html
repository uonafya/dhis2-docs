21.9. Handling multilingual documentation

The DHIS2 docuemtnation has been translated into a number of different languages including French, Spanish and Portuguese. If you would like to create a translation of the documentation or contribute to one of the exisiting translations, please contact the DHIS2 documentation team at the email provided at the end of this chapter.
