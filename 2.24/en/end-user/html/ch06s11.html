6.11. Visualize a chart as a pivot table or a map

When you have made a chart you can switch between pivot table, chart and map visualization of your data.

6.11.1. Open a chart as a pivot table

  1. Click Chart > Open this chart as table.

    Your current chart opens as a pivot table.

6.11.2. Open a chart as a map

  1. Click Chart > Open this chart as map.

    Your current chart opens as a map.
